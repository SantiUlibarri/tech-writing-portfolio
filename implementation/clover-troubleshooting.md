## Common Issues

### Items Not Displaying
**Possible causes:**
- Product sync incomplete
- Network delay

**Resolution:**
- Re-run product sync
- Verify connectivity

---

### Payment Accepted but Not Recorded in ERP
**Possible causes:**
- Integration timeout
- Mapping error

**Resolution:**
- Check transaction logs
- Retry sync

---

### Loyalty Points Not Applied
**Possible causes:**
- Customer not identified
- Rule misconfiguration

**Resolution:**
- Verify loyalty settings
- Confirm customer profile
