# Clover Integration — Customer Journey

## Overview
This document maps the customer and staff journey during a Clover-powered checkout
flow integrated with an ERP system.

---

## Phase 1 — Store Setup

**Actors:** Implementation consultant, Store manager

- Clover device is registered
- ERP connection is validated
- Products and prices are synced
- Loyalty rules are configured

**Outcome:** Store is ready for live transactions

---

## Phase 2 — Customer Checkout

**Actors:** Customer, Cashier

1. Items are scanned on Clover
2. Scanned items appear in real time on the customer-facing display
3. Customer confirms items
4. Loyalty program is identified (login or scan)
5. Available payment methods are shown

**Outcome:** Transparent and fast checkout experience

---

## Phase 3 — Payment & Confirmation

- Payment is processed securely
- ERP records the transaction
- Inventory is updated
- Loyalty points are applied
- Receipt is generated

---

## Phase 4 — Post-Transaction

**Actors:** Support, Operations

- Transaction logs are available
- Errors (if any) are flagged
- Support can trace issues across systems
